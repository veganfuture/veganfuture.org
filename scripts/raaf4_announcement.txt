We’re happy to announce the full program for the fourth edition of the Revolutionary Animal Advocacy Forum (RAAF), taking place on Friday, 22 May 2026 in Amsterdam.

This year, we’re welcoming four inspiring speakers:

Die Radikale Veganerin - Alexandra takes to the streets in her fight for animal liberation. Known as @dieradikaleveganerin, this Berlin-based activist uses confrontation as a deliberate tool, bringing the reality of animal exploitation directly into public space, unapologetically and hard to ignore.

Lammert van Raan - former Member of Parliament for the Party for the Animals, co-editor of Hope in Times of Ecocide, and currently a campaigner for Ecocide Law.

Lenneke van Gaal - a lawyer specializing in trademark law, advertising law, and unlawful publications. Through Stop Humane Washing, she challenges misleading marketing around animal products and exposes how these narratives conceal the realities of animal agriculture.

Kasia Mak - a marketer and strategist on a mission to make our food system healthier, more ethical, and more sustainable. She founded Vegan Girls Club, a female-led community that empowers and supports others on their vegan journeys.

We’ll also be joined by musical guest DINA, and the evening will be moderated by That Chip Guy and Lodewijk Bogaards.

Location: Pakhuis de Zwijger, Amsterdam
Language: English
Cost: Free (signup required)
Optional dinner beforehand available

After the program, there will be time for drinks and socializing.

We look forward to seeing you at RAAF#4!
